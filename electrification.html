JAGUAR ELECTRIFIES

At our workstation, we’re committed to creating battery powered electric cars that are electrifying to drive. Through our Formula E racing team, we’re accelerating the development of electric car technologies, and bringing them to our exhilarating range of battery powered road cars, starting with the I‑PACE Concept.

I‑PACE CONCEPT

I‑PACE Concept is a striking glimpse of our first production battery powered electric car. An All Wheel Drive, 400PS, 5 seater with a range of over 500km*. It achieves 0-60mph in around 4 seconds, and reaches 80% charge in 90 minutes with a 50kW DC rapid charger.

THE RACE TO INNOVATE

To bring electrifying Jaguar performance to our next generation of road cars, we’re competing on the track in the FIA Formula E Championship. It provides us with a real life, fast-track test bed for testing and perfecting electric car technologies.
